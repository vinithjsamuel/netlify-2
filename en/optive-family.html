Why OPTIVE FUSION™?

Optive FUSION™ delivers one drop for all types of DED patients.1

Optive FUSION™ contains hyaluronic acid (HA), which soothes the symptoms of DED.1-3
It works more effectively in combination with CMC, compared to treatments containing CMC alone.1-3
Optive FUSION™ contains carboxymethylcellulose (CMC), 3 which stabilizes the tear film& significantly improves ocular surface integrity compared to HA after 30 days.3
Optive FUSION™ contains osmoprotectants, which help protect the surface of your patients eyes.2
